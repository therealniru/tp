# **The High-Speed CRM for Recruiter Power Users**

Talently is a lightweight, keyboard-optimized desktop application designed for recruiters and hiring managers who handle large volumes of candidates under time pressure. 

![Talently UI](images/Ui.png)

---

## **Why Talently?**

While modern CRMs rely on slow mouse clicks and complex menus, Talently brings the speed of the command line to your candidate pipeline.

### ⚡ **Keyboard-Optimized Control**
Manage your entire database without your hands leaving the keyboard. Add candidates, attach interview notes, and tag specialists in seconds using a powerful yet intuitive Command Line Interface.

### 🔍 **Instant Search & Intelligence**
Locate candidates instantly with partial-match searching across names, contact details, and even deep within interview notes. Never lose a candidate in the noise again.

### 📋 **Complete Candidate Traceability**
Build a professional audit trail for every applicant. Track hiring stages with a flexible tag pool, maintain detailed timestamped notes, and record formal rejection histories.

### 🔒 **Privacy-First & Portable**
Your data stays on your machine. Talently stores everything locally in an open JSON format, ensuring privacy and making data backups as simple as copying a file.
